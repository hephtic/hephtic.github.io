Welcome.

This site serves as a space to document my work and interests at the intersection of mathematics, economics, and data. You'll find personal projects, mathematical simulations, and a curated reading record.

Introduction.

I am a Math-Economics student at UC San Diego interested in the mathematical foundations of economic and financial systems, with a focus on uncertainty, strategic interaction, and stochastic dynamics.

  • Strategic interaction under uncertainty: game theory, mechanism design, information design, and Bayesian inference
  • Stochastic economic and financial modeling: probability theory, financial econometrics, time series, and mathematical finance
  • Analytical foundations: real analysis, measure-theoretic probability, stochastic differential equations, PDEs, and their applications
