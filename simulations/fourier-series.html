Fourier Series Visualization

This interactive simulation demonstrates how any continuous, periodic function can be approximated by a Fourier series; an infinite sum of sine and cosine waves. It supports both real and complex formulations, highlighting the difference between wave-based decompositions and epicycle-based animations.
